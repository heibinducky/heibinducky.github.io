Let's dive into the Universe

Heibin has disappeared, and Ducky is doing his best to find him and bring him back home. Join our penguin friend as he travels through the universe to find our other main protagonist in our very first special, The Travels.

The Travels is our first special, which was set to release a couple weeks after The Heibin & Ducky show was completed. But after some major events happening behind closed doors, we delayed the release until September 2019.

Join our penguin friend in his travels through space!
